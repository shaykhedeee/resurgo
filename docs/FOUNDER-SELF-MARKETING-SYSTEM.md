# Founder Self-Marketing System (Daily Engine)

This is your personal execution framework so marketing happens every day, not “when there is time.”

## Objective

Build trust + authority around Resurgo through consistent founder presence and practical value.

---

## 1) Personal brand positioning

Statement:

> I help people who feel behind build consistency systems that actually stick, using AI + behavior design.

Voice:

- practical
- direct
- supportive
- anti-fluff

Content pillars:

1. Behavior design and habits
2. Real product building lessons
3. AI-assisted productivity workflows
4. User outcomes and case studies

---

## 2) Daily output template (45–90 mins)

### Block A — Capture (10 mins)

Write 3 raw ideas from:

- user conversations
- product friction points
- your own workflow wins/fails

### Block B — Create (20–40 mins)

Produce:

- 1 short post (X + LinkedIn)
- 1 reply thread (5+ thoughtful comments)

### Block C — Distribute (15 mins)

- publish to both platforms
- adapt hook style per platform
- post same core idea in founder community channels

### Block D — Engage (15 mins)

- reply to all comments quickly
- DM warm leads naturally (no hard sell)

---

## 3) Weekly content grid

- Monday: Pain-point post
- Tuesday: Framework post
- Wednesday: Build-in-public update
- Thursday: User story / social proof
- Friday: Contrarian opinion + CTA
- Saturday: Long-form thread / carousel
- Sunday: Weekly recap + next-week challenge

## 5) Founder DM scripts (ethical)

## Warm lead reply

“Appreciate you engaging with my post. Curious—what’s the biggest thing making consistency hard for you right now?”

## Follow-up after reply

“Got it. That’s exactly the problem we’re solving in Resurgo. If helpful, I can send you a free setup flow to build your first goal + habit system in 10 minutes.”

## Soft CTA

“If you want, I can also share the Pro Vision Board Studio demo. It helps people turn vague goals into a visual execution plan.”

---

## 6) Weekly scorecard

Track every week:

- posts published
- impressions
- profile visits
- clicks to site
- signups attributed
- demos/conversations started

Minimum weekly standard:

- 7 posts
- 30 high-quality comments
- 10 meaningful DMs
- 1 long-form educational piece

---

## 7) System rules

- Never skip 2 days in a row
- Prioritize consistency over perfection
- Reuse winning ideas in new formats
- Turn questions into content immediately

---

## 8) AI workflow (free + practical)

Use AI for:

- hook variations
- post rewrites by platform
- comment idea generation
- CTA alternatives
- thread expansion

Never use AI for:

- fake stories
- fake testimonials
- fake metrics

---

## 9) 14-day starter sprint

Days 1–3:

- publish core founder story
- publish 3 pain-point posts

Days 4–7:

- launch mini-series on consistency systems
- collect audience replies for research

Days 8–10:

- publish Vision Board Studio teaser + walkthrough

Days 11–14:

- case study style posts + onboarding CTA
- review scorecard and identify top 3 message angles

Outcome goal:

- create repeatable inbound attention with trust, not hype.
