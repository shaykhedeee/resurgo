# RESURGO Marketing Operating System (MOS) — 2026

## Mission

Build a repeatable growth machine that turns:

- **Awareness → signup**
- **Signup → activated user**
- **Activated user → Pro/Lifetime customer**
- **Customer → referral engine**

This is built for lean execution (solo/few-person team), free/low-cost channels, and measurable output every week.

---

## 1) Growth model (simple + ruthless)

Primary loop:

1. Publish high-value content around pain points (procrastination, habit failure, focus issues)
2. Drive traffic to focused landing pages (`/pricing`, `/features`, `/faq`, `/blog`, `/docs`)
3. Capture intent with clear CTAs + free plan onboarding
4. Activate user in first 24h with quick wins
5. Convert to Pro using high-value moments (Vision Board Studio, advanced analytics, AI coaches)
6. Trigger referrals and social proof loops

### North-star metric

- **Weekly Activated Users** = users who complete:
  - onboarding + first goal + first habit + first focus session

### Revenue metric

- **Free → Pro conversion rate within 14 days**

### Quality metric

- **Week-4 retention (W4)** for new cohorts

## 2) Positioning and messaging matrix

## Core positioning

**Resurgo helps people who feel behind in life become consistent through AI-guided goals, habits, and focus systems.**

### ICP segments

1. **Overwhelmed professionals (25–40)**
   - pain: no system, low consistency
   - hook: “Stop restarting every Monday.”
2. **Students and creators**
   - pain: procrastination + distractions
   - hook: “Turn chaotic days into clear execution.”
3. **Founders/builders**
   - pain: too many priorities, no momentum
   - hook: “Ship weekly with AI accountability.”

### Message pillars

- **Clarity**: Know exactly what to do today
- **Consistency**: Build streaks that survive bad days
- **Identity**: Become the kind of person who follows through
- **Compounding**: Small actions that stack into real outcomes
