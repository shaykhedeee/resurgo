# RESURGO — Backlink Outreach Playbook (Execution OS)

> **Purpose:** Acquire high-quality backlinks that compound domain authority and answer-engine citation eligibility for resurgo.life.
> **Owner:** Founder
> **Cadence:** 3 outreach blocks/week (Tue + Thu + follow-up Fri)
> **Last updated:** 2026-04-20

---

## §1 — North Star KPI + Weekly Targets

**North Star:** Net new quality backlinks acquired per week (editorial, contextual, indexed).

| KPI | Target | Red Line (Escalate If Hit) |
|-----|--------|---------------------------|
| Net new quality links / week | ≥ 4 | < 2 for 2 consecutive weeks |
| First-touch outreach sent / week | ≥ 30 | < 20 |
| Follow-ups sent / week | ≥ 20 | < 10 |
| New qualified prospects added / week | ≥ 40 | < 25 |
| Win rate (links won / emails sent) | ≥ 3% | < 1.5% for 3 weeks |
| Avg DR of won links | ≥ 40 | Consecutive wins below DR 25 |

**8-Week North Star Goal:** 32+ quality backlinks acquired → measurable DA lift → higher answer-engine citation eligibility.

---

## §2 — Target Profile

### Pursue domains with:
- DR/DA relevance and editorial standards
- Audience: productivity, wellness, founder, ADHD, personal growth, SaaS
- Existing listicle / comparison / resource / tool-roundup pages
- Active update cadence (recently refreshed content = higher acceptance rate)

### Never pursue:
- PBNs, link farms, paid placements, or footer spam
- Irrelevant niches
- Domains with toxic backlink profiles
- Sites where outreach goes to `admin@` or `webmaster@` catch-alls

---

## §2.5 — Prospect Source Mix

Use a balanced weekly sourcing mix so outreach quality does not collapse:

| Source | Weekly Share | Why It Matters |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| Competitor backlink mining | 30% | Finds already-proven editors and roundup writers |
| Fresh Google search operators | 25% | Captures recently updated listicles/resources |
| Newsletter/podcast ecosystems | 15% | Higher-trust mentions and secondary amplification |
| Founder + indie communities | 15% | Faster response loop and easier first links |
| Broken-link opportunities | 15% | Often the quickest editorial win |

**Rule:** If one source underperforms for 2 weeks, reduce it by half and reallocate to the best-performing source.

## §2.6 — Target URL Diversity Rules

Do not point every link at the homepage. Weekly target mix should look roughly like this:

| Target Type | Target Share |
|------------|--------------|
| Homepage / core brand page | 20% |
| Comparison pages | 30% |
| High-intent niche pages | 25% |
| Authority guides / research assets | 25% |

This keeps the backlink profile natural and supports both rankings and answer-engine citations.

## §3 — Prospect Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Definition | Exit Criteria |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Sourced | Prospect identified | Valid contact email + relevant page confirmed |
| Qualified | Meets quality threshold | DR relevance + content fit + not a spam domain |
| Contacted | First outreach sent | Email logged with exact send date |
| Follow-up 1 | First follow-up sent | Sent +5 business days after first email |
| Follow-up 2 | Final follow-up sent | Sent +5 business days after FU1 |
| Won | Link secured | Linking URL + anchor + date + DR recorded |
| Lost | No fit / no response after FU2 | Reason logged (no reply / declined / not relevant) |

**Pipeline Health Rule:** If Sourced:Qualified ratio drops below 3:1 for two weeks, switch prospecting source.

## §4 — Outreach Templates

### A) Resource Mention Request

Subject: Quick suggestion for your [resource/list] page

I loved your piece on [topic], especially [specific line].

I noticed you list tools/resources for [use case]. We recently published a practical guide on [specific problem] with actionable frameworks and examples:
[URL]

If you think it helps your readers, would you consider adding it?
Either way, thanks for the great content.

— [Your Name]

### B) Data/Insight Contribution

Subject: Fresh data point for your [article title]

Hi [Name],
Your article on [topic] was super useful.

We have been tracking [metric/behavior] and found [short insight]. If helpful, I can send a clean stat + source line you can include.

Reference: [URL]

Would this be useful for your next update?

— [Your Name]

### C) Broken Link Replacement

Subject: Broken resource on [page title]

Hi [Name],
While reading your page [URL], I noticed a broken link in [section].

If you are updating it, this resource may be a strong replacement:
[URL]

Hope it helps and thanks for curating such a useful page.

— [Your Name]

### D) Guest Contribution Pitch

Subject: Guest contribution idea for [site name]

Hi [Name],
I have been following [site] for a while — your take on [recent post] resonated strongly.

I have a practical angle on [topic] from building Resurgo (AI productivity OS for operators + founders) that I do not think has been covered: [one-sentence hook].

Happy to share a full outline if the angle sounds useful.

— [Your Name]

**Template Performance Log:** After every 50 sends, measure response rate per template. Kill templates below 2% response. A/B subject lines every 4 weeks.

## §5 — Quality Threshold for Accepted Links

Accept only if ALL of the following are true:
- Page is contextually relevant to Resurgo's audience
- Domain is indexed and not penalty-flagged
- Link is editorially placed (not in a widget, footer, or sidebar spam block)
- Anchor text looks natural and matches context
- DR >= 25 (exceptions at DR 15-24 only if audience is highly targeted)

### Record for every won link:

| Field | Record |
|-------|--------|
| Linking URL | full URL |
| Target URL | Resurgo page linked |
| Anchor text | exact text |
| Domain DR | number |
| Date acquired | YYYY-MM-DD |
| Outreach angle used | template A / B / C / D |
| Notes | editorial context |

## §7 — Action Playbooks

### Scenario A: Win rate drops below 1.5% for 2 consecutive weeks
1. Audit last 50 sent emails — what angle, what subject, what target?
2. Kill lowest-performing template.
3. Write one new variant with different subject line and value hook.
4. Switch 50% of outreach to broken-link replacement (highest baseline response rate).
5. Re-qualify prospect list — tighten relevance filter.

### Scenario B: Prospects added drops below 25 in a week
1. Open one new sourcing channel (Ahrefs "Best by links" report, BuzzSumo, or fresh Google searches for `[niche] + "best tools"`).
2. Mine 3 competitor backlink profiles for untapped prospects.
3. Revisit "Lost — no reply" list from 60+ days ago for re-engagement.

### Scenario C: Won links are all low DR (< 30)
1. Raise qualification bar — require DR >= 40 for new prospects.
2. Shift effort to guest contribution angle (higher DR ceiling).
3. Pitch 3 newsletter placements + 2 podcast appearances this week.
4. Target roundup authors directly (Google: "best productivity apps 2026").

---

## §8 — Compounding Strategy (Every 2 Weeks)

1. Identify top-linked content format from won links (comparison, how-to, checklist, data study).
2. Publish one new asset in that format on resurgo.life/blog.
3. Re-run outreach campaign using that asset as the reference.
4. Re-engage non-responders from 30+ days ago with the new asset.
5. Log which asset earns the most links — build next asset in the same format.

**Compounding Rule:** Every won link is a signal to create more of the same content type.

## §10 — QA Guardrails

Before any email goes out, verify:
- [ ] Personalization field [Name] is filled (never send "Hi [Name]")
- [ ] Reference URL is live (not 404)
- [ ] Prospect domain is indexed and not spam-flagged
- [ ] Anchor text suggestion is natural (not exact-match keyword stuffing)

- [ ] Outreach CRM entry is updated before sending

## §11 — Definition of Done

Weekly outreach block is **Done** when:

- [ ] Prospect count logged in scorecard
- [ ] All first-touch emails recorded with send date
- [ ] All follow-ups sent on schedule (+5 biz days)
- [ ] All won links fully recorded (URL + anchor + DR + date)
- [ ] Scorecard updated and blocker + fix logged
- [ ] Next week's prospect batch pre-qualified (>= 40 ready)
