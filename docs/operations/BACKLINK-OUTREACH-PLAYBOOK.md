# RESURGO — Backlink Outreach Playbook (Execution)

> Purpose: Acquire high-quality backlinks that improve authority and answer-engine citation likelihood.
> Owner: Founder + marketing assistant
> Cadence: 3 outreach blocks/week

---

## 1) Target Profile (what to pursue)

Prioritize domains with:
- DR/DA relevance (quality over volume)
- Productivity, wellness, founder, ADHD, or personal growth audience
- Existing listicle/comparison/resource pages
- Real editorial standards (avoid spam directories)

Avoid:
- PBNs, link farms, paid low-quality placements
- Irrelevant niches

---

## 2) Prospect Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Definition | Exit Criteria |
|------|------------|---------------|
| Sourced | Prospect identified | Valid contact + relevant page found |
| Qualified | Meets quality threshold | Relevance + likely fit confirmed |
| Contacted | First outreach sent | Email logged with date |
| Follow-up 1 | First follow-up sent | +5 business days after first email |
| Follow-up 2 | Final follow-up sent | +5 business days after follow-up 1 |
| Won | Link secured | URL + anchor + date recorded |
| Lost | No fit/no response | Marked with reason |

---

## 3) Minimum Weekly Targets

- 40 new qualified prospects
- 30 first-touch outreach emails
- 20 follow-ups
- 4+ net new quality links

---

## 4) Outreach Templates

### A) Resource Mention Request
Subject: Quick suggestion for your [resource/list] page

Hi [Name],
I loved your piece on [topic], especially [specific line].

I noticed you list tools/resources for [use case]. We recently published a practical guide on [specific problem] with actionable frameworks and examples:
[URL]

If you think it helps your readers, would you consider adding it?
Either way, thanks for the great content.

— [Your Name]

### B) Data/Insight Contribution
Subject: Fresh data point for your [article title]

Hi [Name],
Your article on [topic] was super useful.

We’ve been tracking [metric/behavior] and found [short insight]. If helpful, I can send a clean stat + source line you can include.

Reference: [URL]

Would this be useful for your next update?

— [Your Name]

### C) Broken Link Replacement
Subject: Broken resource on [page title]

Hi [Name],
While reading your page [URL], I noticed a broken link in [section].

If you’re updating it, this resource may be a strong replacement:
[URL]

Hope it helps and thanks for curating such a useful page.

— [Your Name]

---

## 5) Quality Threshold for Accepted Links

Accept only if:
- Contextually relevant page
- Indexed domain
- Editorially placed (not hidden/footer spam)
- Anchor text looks natural

Track for every win:
- Linking URL
- Target URL
- Anchor text
- Domain quality notes
- Date acquired

---

## 6) Weekly Reporting Block

Report every Friday:
- New prospects added
- Emails sent
- Follow-ups sent
- Links won
- Win rate (%)
- Top-performing outreach angle
- Blockers + next week fixes

---

## 7) Compounding Strategy

Every 2 weeks:
1. Identify top-linked content format (comparison, how-to, checklist).
2. Publish one new asset in that format.
3. Re-run outreach with improved asset.
4. Re-engage past non-responders with a stronger angle.
