## First Comment (Founder Story)

> Hey Product Hunt — I built Resurgo after watching myself fail at every productivity system I tried.
>
> Not because the systems were bad. Because they assumed I would show up every day with full energy, perfect memory, and no competing priorities. No system does that.
>
> I wanted an app that:
> - Accepts a brain dump and turns it into a prioritized plan in 60 seconds
> - Has AI coaches that adapt to your energy, not your theoretical best self
> - Treats a missed day as data, not a personal failure
> - Costs less than one coffee per month (free tier genuinely free, no card required)
>
> Five coaches: Marcus (Stoic Strategist), Titan (Performance), Aurora (Wellness), Phoenix (Comeback), Nexus (Integration). Each trained on a different philosophy. Each usable in the free tier for core habits.
>
> Built with Next.js, Convex, Groq (llama-3.3-70b), Clerk auth, and Dodo Payments.
>
> Would love your feedback — especially from anyone who has tried and abandoned 3+ productivity apps this year. That is exactly who this was built for.
